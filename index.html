ABOUT NIGHT WALKERS

In the village of Kasukawi, a young boy named Korma is scared. He is terrified because his friend Sal told him about the possibility of zombies taking over their town. Korma, with his limited knowledge of this weird creature, is guiding his little sister Anna to protect herself. All he knows is that zombies represent frightening realities - namely the dangers of viral infections, anger and violence and death by trauma. He is also aware of the fact that a zombie turns its victims into mindless cannibals. In this state, zombies don't die even when fatally wounded or after being shot, blown up or cut open. They keep coming back for more.

The apocalyptic predictions have made many people wary of waking up on the wrong side of this petrifying event. The head of the village Gash has declared a state of emergency. Schools have declared lockdowns or closings during the night so students can get some sleep. Additionally, parents are instructed to keep their children in constant contact with them, so no one is alone at night. All health workers should wear protective gear when dealing with zombies in case someone turns deadly again. Although only theoretical at this point, scientists are working on ways to reverse the effects of a zombie bite so its victims can die peacefully.

Before a solution could be found, the apocalypse happened. Hundreds of zombies invaded the village and wiped off more than half of the town’s population. Even the influential Gash was caught by one of the zombies and got infected by the virus. They even invaded the judicial offices and the parliament. With everything on a complete shutdown, the whole town is at a standstill. Everyone has become even more cautious, not even going out for basic supplies rather relying on their previous stocks.

During all this chaos, zombies had herded around the house where Korma and his little sister Anna were living along with their parents. With a heroic attempt by his friend Sal, he managed to save the siblings while their parents fell victim at the hands of the ruthless zombies. Extremely emotional and scared, the 3 set out on a journey away from the zombies on the family car.

They managed to get rid of the herd just on the outskirts of town where they soon found a cave to settle in. With limited resources available and the cries of people getting closer to their safe haven, they blocked the entrance with the car. Upon searching inside the cave, they salvaged some food from previous inhabitants of the place and luckily a cell phone through which they were able to reach out to a police officer in town. They explained their situation and pleaded for the Nightwalkers to come to their rescue.

The Nightwalkers, a group of heroic individuals who choose to revolt against the threat of zombies taking over the world, use fire and sulfur gas to fend off the zombies. This technique alters the brain chemistry of the zombie by quenching their thirst of feeding on humans. With high hopes and a bunch of fearless individuals, they have set out on their quest to extinguish this plague once and for all.

Along with Sal, Korma and his sister, many others are stranded in the similar situation surrounded by zombies with little to no hope of survival and they need urgent help. The Nightwalker community is reaching out to the people of the world to step up and contribute in this cause against fighting this epidemic.
